Apple Inc. Q3 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: July 31, 2025

Tim Cook, CEO:

Good afternoon, everyone. We had a productive June quarter with revenue of $94 billion. Our gross margin was 46.5%, and we continue to see the positive impact of our Services segment on overall profitability.

iPhone demand remained healthy, and we're very excited about the upcoming product cycle. We spent $8.9 billion on R&D this quarter as we accelerate our investment in artificial intelligence and machine learning capabilities.

Kevan Parekh, CFO:

Thank you, Tim.

Revenue for Q3 was $94 billion. Gross profit was $43.7 billion. Operating income came in at $28.2 billion, and net income was $23.4 billion. Diluted EPS was $1.57.

EBITDA was $31 billion for the quarter.

Cash from operations was $27.9 billion, and free cash flow was $24.4 billion. We spent $3.5 billion in capital expenditures.

We repurchased $21.1 billion of our common stock and paid $3.9 billion in dividends during the quarter.

On the balance sheet, we had $36.3 billion in cash, total assets of $331.5 billion, and total debt of $101.7 billion. Stockholders' equity stood at $65.8 billion.

Analyst Q&A:

Analyst: The gross margin seems to have dipped slightly from Q2. What's driving that?

Kevan Parekh: Our gross margin of 46.5% compares to 47.1% in Q2. This is primarily due to seasonal product mix, which is typical for the June quarter. The underlying fundamentals remain strong.
