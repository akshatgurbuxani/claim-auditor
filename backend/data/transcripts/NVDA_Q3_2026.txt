NVIDIA Corporation Q3 FY2026 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: November 20, 2025

Jensen Huang, CEO:

Thank you everyone for joining us. We delivered another record quarter. Revenue was $57 billion, reflecting incredible demand for our data center GPU platforms. Our gross margin was 73.4%, and gross profit was $41.8 billion.

The data center business continues to see unprecedented demand driven by the AI revolution. Our Blackwell platform is ramping strongly, and we're seeing broad adoption across hyperscalers, enterprise, and sovereign AI initiatives.

Colette Kress, CFO:

Revenue of $57 billion was a new record. Operating income reached $36 billion, and net income was $31.9 billion. Diluted earnings per share were $1.30.

Operating cash flow was strong and free cash flow remained robust.

On the balance sheet, we maintain a very strong position to continue investing in next-generation AI platforms.
