Apple Inc. Q2 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: May 1, 2025

Tim Cook, CEO:

Good afternoon. We're pleased to report strong results for the March quarter with revenue of $95.4 billion. Our gross margin was 47.1%, reflecting the strength of our product portfolio and growing Services contribution.

We generated gross profit of $44.9 billion this quarter. Our team continues to execute well across all product categories. We invested $8.6 billion in research and development during the quarter.

Kevan Parekh, CFO:

Revenue was $95.4 billion for the quarter. Operating income was $29.6 billion, and net income was $24.8 billion. Diluted EPS came in at $1.65.

EBITDA was $32.2 billion.

Operating cash flow was $24 billion and free cash flow was $20.9 billion, with capital expenditures of $3.1 billion.

During the quarter, we returned capital through $25.9 billion in share repurchases and $3.8 billion in dividends.

On the balance sheet, cash was $28.2 billion, total assets were $331.2 billion, and total debt was $98.2 billion. Stockholders' equity was $66.8 billion.
