Microsoft Corporation Q4 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: July 22, 2025

Satya Nadella, CEO:

Thank you, and good afternoon. We closed out fiscal year 2025 with an exceptional fourth quarter. Revenue was $76.4 billion, and our gross margin was 68.6%, resulting in gross profit of $52.4 billion.

Cloud is the central driver of our results. Azure continues to gain share and our AI services are contributing meaningfully to overall growth.

Amy Hood, CFO:

Revenue of $76.4 billion reflected broad-based growth. Operating income was $34.3 billion, and net income reached $27.2 billion. Diluted EPS was $3.65.

Operating cash flow was a very strong $42.6 billion, and free cash flow came in at $25.6 billion.

We ended the fiscal year with $30.2 billion in cash, total assets of $619 billion, and total debt of $60.6 billion. Stockholders' equity was $343.5 billion.
