Apple Inc. Q1 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: January 30, 2025

Tim Cook, CEO:

Good afternoon. Thank you for joining us. We're thrilled to report record holiday quarter results. Revenue for the first quarter of fiscal 2025 was $124.3 billion, a new all-time record for Apple.

Our gross margin was 46.9%, and gross profit reached $58.3 billion. We continue to see incredible momentum across our entire product lineup, particularly iPhone and Services.

We invested $8.3 billion in R&D this quarter, reflecting our ongoing commitment to innovation across hardware, software, and services.

Luca Maestri, CFO:

Thank you, Tim.

Total revenue of $124.3 billion set a new record. Operating income was $42.8 billion, and net income reached $36.3 billion. Diluted earnings per share were $2.40.

EBITDA for the quarter was $45.9 billion, demonstrating the strength of our business model.

We generated $29.9 billion in operating cash flow and $27 billion in free cash flow. Capital expenditures were $2.9 billion.

We returned approximately $27.5 billion to shareholders, including $23.6 billion in share repurchases and $3.9 billion in dividends.

The balance sheet remains incredibly strong. We ended the quarter with $30.3 billion in cash and cash equivalents. Total assets were $344.1 billion, total liabilities were $277.3 billion, and total debt was $96.8 billion. Stockholders' equity was $66.8 billion.

Analyst Q&A:

Analyst: Tim, can you comment on the revenue growth trajectory?

Tim Cook: Revenue of $124.3 billion grew from $119.6 billion a year ago, which is roughly 4% growth year over year. We're seeing broad-based strength across all geographies and product categories.
