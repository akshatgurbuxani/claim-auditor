Apple Inc. Q4 FY2025 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: October 30, 2025

Tim Cook, CEO:

Good afternoon. We're pleased to close out fiscal year 2025 with a strong fourth quarter. Revenue for the quarter was $102.5 billion, representing solid growth. Our gross margin was 47.2%, and gross profit was $48.3 billion.

We continue to see strength across our ecosystem. iPhone remains the cornerstone of our product portfolio, and our installed base continues to grow. Mac and iPad also showed resilience in the quarter.

Our R&D spending was $8.9 billion this quarter, as we continue investing in Apple Intelligence, our silicon roadmap, and spatial computing technologies.

Kevan Parekh, CFO:

Let me provide the financial details for Q4.

Revenue was $102.5 billion. Operating income was $32.4 billion and net income was $27.5 billion. Diluted earnings per share were $1.85.

EBITDA came in at $35.9 billion for the quarter.

On cash flow, we generated $29.7 billion in cash from operations and $26.5 billion in free cash flow. Capital expenditures were approximately $3.2 billion.

We returned significant capital to shareholders, with $20.1 billion in share repurchases and $3.9 billion in dividends during the quarter.

The balance sheet remains strong. We ended the quarter with $35.9 billion in cash, total assets of $359.2 billion, total liabilities of $285.5 billion, and total debt of $112.4 billion. Stockholders' equity was $73.7 billion.

Analyst Q&A:

Analyst: Can you speak to the free cash flow performance?

Kevan Parekh: Free cash flow of $26.5 billion was very strong. We continue to generate substantial cash that allows us to invest in the business while returning capital to shareholders.
