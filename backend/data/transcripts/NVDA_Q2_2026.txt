NVIDIA Corporation Q2 FY2026 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: August 28, 2025

Jensen Huang, CEO:

Good afternoon. We delivered outstanding results in Q2 with revenue of $46.7 billion. Our gross margin was 72.4%, with gross profit of $33.9 billion.

AI demand continues to accelerate across all market segments. Our data center business is benefiting from the buildout of AI infrastructure globally.

Colette Kress, CFO:

Revenue of $46.7 billion was well above expectations. Operating income was $28.4 billion, and net income reached $26.4 billion. Diluted EPS was $1.08.

Analyst Q&A:

Analyst: Can you discuss the gross margin trends?

Colette Kress: Gross margin of 72.4% reflects the product mix shift. As Blackwell ramps, we expect some variability in gross margins quarter to quarter, but we remain focused on maintaining strong profitability.
