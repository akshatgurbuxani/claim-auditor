Microsoft Corporation Q1 FY2026 Earnings Call Transcript
Date: October 29, 2025

Satya Nadella, CEO:

Good afternoon everyone. We delivered strong first quarter results with revenue of $77.7 billion. Our gross margin remained healthy at 69%, with gross profit of $53.6 billion.

We're seeing tremendous momentum in our AI business. Azure revenue growth continued to accelerate, powered by both traditional cloud workloads and increasing AI demand. Our Copilot products are being adopted by enterprises of all sizes.

Amy Hood, CFO:

Total revenue was $77.7 billion. Operating income was $38 billion, and net income was $27.7 billion. Diluted EPS was $3.72.

We generated $45.1 billion in operating cash flow and $25.7 billion in free cash flow.

Our balance sheet remains strong with $28.8 billion in cash, total assets of $636.4 billion, and total debt of $60.6 billion. Total stockholders' equity was $363.1 billion.
